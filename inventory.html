How to Use This Tool

  • Add New Item — Click the big button. New items start blank and appear at the top until you assign a category.
  • Edit an Item — Tap "Edit". The edit card replaces the item in its exact position.
  • Stock Colors — Stock number turns yellow at 10 or less, orange at 5 or less, red at 0.
  • Time on Menu Colors — "On menu" text changes: green (fresh), yellow (8-14 days), orange (15-30 days), red (30+ days — needs attention).
  • Last Edited — Shows when you last saved changes to the item.
  • Profit Since Last Update — Tracks sales from changes you make in this session only.
  • All-Time Sales History — Persistent history with line chart and pie chart (switch tabs).
  • Export JSON — Click this after every session. This is how you save your data.
